Useful Formulas

Sum of consecutive numbers in base P

$$ \sum_{i=0}^{n} P^i = \frac{p^{n+1}-1}{P-1} $$ This counts the number of nodes in a tree of depth n with fanout P.

Depth and number of leaves in tree, given number of nodes and fanout

$$ d = \log_f(2|v|+1)-1 $$
$$ |leaves| = f^d $$

Sum of consecutive integers

$$ \sum_{n=1}^{k} n = \frac{n(n+1)}{2} $$
